NDModulE Controller Card Emulator

Controller card for HDD emulated in hardware using a RP2350 MicroController for disk drive emulation with SD card storage. Early prototype stage with planned features including MFM emulation, SCSI emulation, and floppy controller support. Currently undergoing redesign to fix design issues.
