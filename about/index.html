Our Mission

RetroCore Labs is dedicated to keeping classic computing alive through modern emulation, FPGA implementations, and hardware projects. We believe in preserving computing history while making it accessible to new generations.

Our projects range from accurate emulators of classic systems to complete FPGA reimplementations, focusing particularly on Norsk Data systems and other historically significant platforms.

Core Values

Open Source

We believe in transparent, collaborative development. All our projects are open source, allowing community participation and learning.

Accuracy

Our implementations strive for cycle-accurate emulation and authentic hardware behavior, preserving the original experience.

Education

We document our work extensively, creating learning resources for future generations of developers and enthusiasts.
